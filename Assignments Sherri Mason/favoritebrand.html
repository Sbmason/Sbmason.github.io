Favorite Brand 

When looking through my favorite brands for fashion inspiration, I usually turn to Nike. Nike is primarily known as one of the most popular clothing/shoe brands. Nike doesn’t only make outfit shoes. Nike also makes shoes and clothes for running or playing in cold or warm weather, indoor/outdoor athletics, and shoes for almost every athletic occasion. Even though this may not be the best place to find dressy attire inspiration, once you get on their site or go in store, you can see that the content creators are the essential parts of the site or store. 

Why Nike?

Nike provides a variety of options when it comes to athletic wear. What makes them special is their ability to create custom shoes and attire for different professional athletes and regular people to allow them to compete in almost any weather condition. Nike’s font choice can be mistaken for a regular sanserif font. Nike’s primary colors include black backgrounds with white text or vice versa.

Nike doesn’t have a primary background so the background could be many colors, but the text is almost always white or black. The change of color in the shoes/apparel usually come from Nike being inspired by a team or an individual player. Most of the designs include neutral colors so the shoe/outfit can go with anything. This allows Nike to stand out in the fashion industry, making the brand more appealing to potential buyers that are searching for good quality and variety. 

What do I Plan to Use?

I really like the combination of fonts used by Nike. One semi-standard sanserif font with high weight. The font used for everything looks a little slanted, chunky, and with a high weight. I plan to use the same fonts as Nike. They chose Google Fonts. This will my font “Futura Extra Bold Condensed.” I plan on copying the black background and white text approach as well as the different color approach. Nike has a logo, which is said to have been using “Futura Extra Bold Condensed.” 

My Logo Header 

For the logo header, I plan to try to keep it pretty simple and try to emphasize the meaning of the website and store: Athletic shoe/ clothing apparel. So, I plan to keep much white on black and different variations of colors as possible. There are couple of ways that this can happen. I didn’t decide on a header yet, but my options borrow black, red, blue and white.
